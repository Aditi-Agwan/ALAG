Welcome to ALAG!

ALAG, an online application, represents an effort to help to interact with their classmates and with
their academic lives.It provides a Micro-blogging platform with additional emoticon feature to help
readers understand message, connect with other students and have productive interaction, dynamic
calendar that contains events like job fairs and college fest along with all the necessary requirements
and a book recommender module to list books suggested by professors.
